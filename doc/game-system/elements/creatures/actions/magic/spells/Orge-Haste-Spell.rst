Haste Spell
...........


Suggested In-Game Names
_______________________

This spell could be named:

 - fantasy setting: ``Haste`` (english) / ``Diligence`` or ``Accélération`` (french)
 


Spell Effects 
_____________

The target will be able to perform more actions per unit of time, and will be less fatigued by them.

Available only in the Cleric path, through gesture spellcasting.

See also: `Slow Spell`_.
