Cure Light Wounds Spell
.......................


Suggested In-Game Names
_______________________

This spell could be named:

 - fantasy setting: ``Cure Light Wounds`` or ``Soothing Balm``(english) / ``Guérison des blessures légères`` or ``Baume apaisant`` (french)



Spell Effects 
_____________

In case of success, relieves immediately and at least partially the target from a minor wound or paralysis, light poisoning or a non-lethal disease.

Cures, but does not heal.

Available in the Mage path, through gesture spellcasting, in the Cleric path, through incantation, gesture and rune spellcasting, and in the Bard path, through bardic verse spellcasting.

See also: `Cure Major Wounds Spell`_, `Mass Curing Spell`_, and `Heal Spell`_.
