Darkness of the Depths Spell
............................


Suggested In-Game Names
_______________________


This spell could be named:

 - fantasy setting: ``Darkness`` or ``Darkness of the Depths`` (english) / ``Obscurité`` or ``Obscurité des profondeurs`` (french)


Spell Effects 
_____________

Hides most if not all light sources in a zone. Not able to hide the direct light of the sun, and less effective on magical lights.

Available only in the Mage path, through incantation, gesture or pure mental spellcasting.
