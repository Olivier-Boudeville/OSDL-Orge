Suggested In-Game Names
_______________________


This spell could be named:

 - fantasy setting: ``Detect Cursed`` (english) / ``Détection des malédictions`` (french)



Spell Effects 
_____________

When cast on an object or creature, the caster may be able to gather some knowledge about any curse still operating on it: what are its effects, regarding which deity the curse was made, possibly who made it, etc.

Available only in the Cleric path, through incantation, gesture or pure mental spellcasting.
