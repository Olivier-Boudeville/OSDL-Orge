Uncage Spell
............


Suggested In-Game Names
_______________________


This spell could be named:

 - fantasy setting: ``Uncage`` or ``Unlock`` (english) / ``Forçage de serrures`` or ``Déverrouillage`` (french)


Spell Effects 
_____________

Tries to open items (chests, doors, etc.) locked by non-arcane means. 

Some unverified claims reported that sometimes this spell made small yet nasty and weird creatures pop into the closed area.

Available only in the Mage path, through incantation or rune spellcasting.
