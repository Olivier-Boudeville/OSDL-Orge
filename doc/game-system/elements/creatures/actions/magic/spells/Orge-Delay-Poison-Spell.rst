Delay Poison Spell
..................


Suggested In-Game Names
_______________________


This spell could be named:

 - fantasy setting: ``Delay Poison`` (english) / ``Empoisonnement retardé`` (french)



Spell Effects 
_____________

Once cast on a group of creatures, any future poisoning will have its effects lessen and postponed, leaving them more time to be cured.

Particularly useful in areas infested with poisonous species.

Available only in the Bard path, through bardic verse spellcasting.
