Harmony Offer Spell
...................


Suggested In-Game Names
_______________________


This spell could be named:

 - ``Harmony Offer`` (english) / ``Concorde Proposée`` (french)



Spell Effects 
_____________

Calms the target if it is aggressive, towards the caster or not. 

Helps also to tame all kinds of wild animals.

Mostly efficient when used on NPC creatures which are not characters.

Available only in the Bard path, through bardic verse spellcasting.
