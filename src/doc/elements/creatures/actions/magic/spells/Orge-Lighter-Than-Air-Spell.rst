Lighter Than Air Spell
......................


Suggested In-Game Names
_______________________

This spell could be named:

 - ``Lighter Than Air`` (english) / ``Lévitation Douce`` (french)



Spell Effects 
_____________

Makes an object or a creature levitate, during a given period of time and with a given speed.

Only the caster is able to control, to some extent only, where the levitating object or creature will travel.

Resisting this spell is fairly easy.
