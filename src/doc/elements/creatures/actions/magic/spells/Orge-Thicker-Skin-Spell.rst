Thicker Skin Spell
..................


Suggested In-Game Names
_______________________


This spell could be named:

 - fantasy setting: ``Thicker Skin`` or ``Skin Armor`` (english) / ``Peau d'écaille`` or ``Armure de peau`` (french)



Spell Effects 
_____________

The skin of target creature gets thicker and thicker, until forming a kind of strong crust that increases its protection. 

Large clothes should be worn if planning to be the target of that spell.

Can be used as well not to be recognized by others, as the spell blurs the features of the target.

Available only in the Mage path, through incantation, gesture or rune spellcasting.
