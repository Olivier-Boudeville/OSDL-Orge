Suggested In-Game Names
_______________________

This spell could be named:

 - ``Cure Light Wounds`` or ``Soothing Balm``(english) / ``Guérison des blessures légères`` or ``Baume apaisant`` (french)



Spell Effects 
_____________

In case of success, relieves immediately and at least partially from a minor wound or paralysis, light poisoning or a non-lethal disease.
