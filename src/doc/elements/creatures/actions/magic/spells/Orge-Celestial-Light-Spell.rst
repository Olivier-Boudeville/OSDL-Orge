Celestial Light Spell
.....................


Suggested In-Game Names
_______________________


This spell could be named:

 - fantasy setting: ``Celestial Light`` (english) / ``Lumière céleste`` (french)



Spell Effects 
_____________

This spell results into a strong uniform lighting around the casting point, casting no shadow and lasting for quite long.

Some objects are said to be invisible under this lighting, whereas the light of sun discover them all.

Available only in the Cleric path, through incantation, gesture, rune or pure mental spellcasting.

In the case of rune spellcasting, the light will follow the object or creature on which the rune was affixed.

See also: `Aurora Borealis Spell`_.
