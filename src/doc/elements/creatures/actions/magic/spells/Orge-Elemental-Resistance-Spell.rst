Elemental Resistance Spell
..........................


Suggested In-Game Names
_______________________


This spell could be named:

 - fantasy setting: ``Elemental Resistance`` (english) / ``Résistance Elémentaire`` (french)



Spell Effects 
_____________

The target benefits from an increased resistance against elemental damage.

Available in the Mage path, through gesture spellcasting, in the Cleric path, through rune spellcasting, and in the Bard path, through bardic verse spellcasting (in this case the whole party of the caster will be affected).
