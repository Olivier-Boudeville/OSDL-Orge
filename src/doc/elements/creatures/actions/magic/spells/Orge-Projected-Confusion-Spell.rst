Projected Confusion Spell
.........................


Suggested In-Game Names
_______________________

This spell could be named:

 - ``Projected Confusion`` (english) / ``Confusion Projetée`` (french)



Spell Effects 
_____________

All the creatures in an area - especially advanced sentient beings - that fail to resist the spell are affected by a period of irrationality and a lack of conscious reasoning, with lasts a few moments.

Most of these creatures will end up acting absurdly, colliding with the surrounding creatures and objects, or remaining paralyzed, sometimes drooling.

The caster can be affected as well.
