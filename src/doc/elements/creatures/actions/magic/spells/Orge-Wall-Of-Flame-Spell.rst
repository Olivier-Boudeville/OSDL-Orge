Wall Of Flame Spell
...................


Suggested In-Game Names
_______________________

This spell could be named:

 - fantasy setting: ``Wall Of Flame`` (english) / ``Mur de Feu`` or ``Barrière Infernale`` (french)



Spell Effects 
_____________

In front of the caster, a wall of flames appears, inflicting fire damage to any creature going through it proportionally to its flame density.

The more this spell is powerfully cast, the bigger the wall will be (in length, width and height), the longer it will last, and the higher its flame density will be.
