Unshackle Mind Spell
....................


Suggested In-Game Names
_______________________

This spell could be named:

 - fantasy setting: ``Unshackle Mind`` or ``Remove Influence`` (english) / ``Restauration de libre arbitre`` or ``Libération d'emprise mentale`` (french)



Spell Effects 
_____________

This spell attempts to free the mind of the target creature from any external influence, or to lessen its effects.

See also: `Control Of Easily Influenced Creatures Spell`_, `Kind Suggestion Spell`_ and `Command Creature Spell`_.

Available only in the Cleric path, through incantation, rune, bardic verse or pure mental spellcasting.
