Mass Curing Spell
.................


Suggested In-Game Names
_______________________

This spell could be named:

 - fantasy setting: ``Mass Curing`` or ``Mass Recovery`` (english) / ``Guérison de tous`` (french)



Spell Effects 
_____________

Relieves all creatures in the target area from a wound, paralysis, poisoning or disease.
	
Cures, but does not heal.

Less effective than `Cure Major Wounds Spell`_, but may take care of multiple creatures.

Available only in the Cleric path, through incantation spellcasting.

See also: `Cure Light Wounds Spell`_, `Cure Major Wounds Spell`_ and `Heal Spell`_.
