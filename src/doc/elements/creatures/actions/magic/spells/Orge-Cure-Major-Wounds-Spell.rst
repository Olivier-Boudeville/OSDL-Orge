Suggested In-Game Names
_______________________

This spell could be named:

 - fantasy setting: ``Cure Major Wounds`` or ``Miracle Recovery``(english) / ``Guérison des blessures graves`` or ``Guérison Miraculeuse`` (french)



Spell Effects 
_____________

In case of success, relieves from a wound or paralysis, serious poisoning or a potentially lethal disease.

This spell has been reported in some cases to transform the source of suffering into another one.
	
Cures, but does not heal.

Available only in the Cleric path, through incantation, gesture, rune or pure mental spellcasting.

See also: `Cure Light Wounds Spell`_, `Mass Curing Spell`_, and `Heal Spell`_.
