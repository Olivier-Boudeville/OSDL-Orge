Detect Magic Spell
..................


Suggested In-Game Names
_______________________


This spell could be named:

 - fantasy setting: ``Detect Magic`` or ``Magical Potential Scouting`` (english) / ``Détection de la Magie`` or ``Détection de Potentiel Magique`` (french)


Spell Effects 
_____________

This spells allows to gather some - often evasive - indication about the presence of magic, i.e. links with the Arcanes. 

Can operate on objects, creatures and places.

It is often used for the evaluation of artifacts, the search of hidding places and the detection of hostile creatures.

Available only in the Mage path, through incantation, gesture, rune or pure mental spellcasting.
