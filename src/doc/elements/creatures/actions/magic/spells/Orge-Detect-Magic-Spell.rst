Detect Magic Spell
..................

Suggested In-Game Names
_______________________


This spell could be named:

 - fantasy setting: ``Detect Magic`` / ``Magical Potential Scouting`` (english) / ``Détection de Potentiel Magique`` (french)


Spell Effects 
_____________

This spells allows to gather some - often evasive - indication about the presence of magic.

It is often used for the evaluation of artifacts, the search of hidding places and the detection of hostile creatures.
